While we’re all trying to tackle mental health problems,
here is the criteria that our judges are looking for!

The goal is to create a proof-of-concept with working parts–code or hardware that can be tested after the hackathon.

Innovation / Creativity

Does this project solve a problem in an interesting or effective way?

Technical Challenge

How technically difficult or interesting is the project?

Usability

The experience of using the product –
How easy is it to use?
Is it desirable or nice to use? Would a person with mental health challenges want to use it?

Impact

Does this solve the problem?
How much will it affect someone with mental health challenges?
How much will it improve quality of life?

Ready for Launch

How easily can this project be launched or implemented?

ABOUT US

Design Lab is a nonprofit organization that is based in Vancouver. Our vision is to integrate health and tech to create a community that achieves more in both areas. We host tech events in Vancouver that teach the technical aspects of up and coming technology and create workshops to teach and develop new skills.
